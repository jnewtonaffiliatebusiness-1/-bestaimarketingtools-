In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** Best CRM for teams who want simplicity and great support without sacrificing sales features

## Key Features

Nutshell offers a suite of features focused on its core crm sales automation use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — excellent customer support.
- **Integrations:** Good pipeline management.
- **Reporting:** Email marketing included.
- **Usability:** Generally well-designed with a reasonable learning curve..

## Pricing

Nutshell starts at **$19/month**. Pricing scales based on number of users and contacts.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Limited advanced automation
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount

## Who Is Nutshell For?

**Best for:**
