## What Is Mailchimp?

Mailchimp is a email marketing tool designed to help businesses build and engage their email audience. The original email platform.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** The original email platform — fine for beginners but outgrown by most serious marketers by year two

## Key Features

Mailchimp offers a suite of features focused on its core email marketing use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — easy to start — good free plan.
- **Integrations:** Wide integration ecosystem.
- **Reporting:** Good landing page builder.
- **Usability:** Requires patience — the UI has rough edges that slow workflows..

## Pricing

Mailchimp starts at **$13/month**. Pricing scales based on contact count and sending volume.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Automation is basic vs. competitors
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount

## Who Is Mailchimp For?

**Best for:**
- Teams that prioritize easy capabilities
- Businesses already using complementary tools in the same ecosystem
- Organizations with the technical resources to manage the significant learning curve

**Not ideal for:**
- Pricing becomes expensive fast situations
- Teams looking for an all-in-one solution
- Businesses that want simpler, more direct value from their tools
