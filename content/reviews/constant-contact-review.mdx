## What Is Constant Contact?

Constant Contact is a email marketing tool designed to help businesses build and engage their email audience. Long-established but now clearly outdated.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** Long-established but now clearly outdated — struggling to compete with modern, cheaper alternatives

## Key Features

Constant Contact offers a suite of features focused on its core email marketing use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — easy to use.
- **Integrations:** Solid event management features.
- **Reporting:** Good support.
- **Usability:** Requires patience — the UI has rough edges that slow workflows..

## Pricing

Constant Contact starts at **$12/month**. Pricing scales based on contact count and sending volume.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Basic automation
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount

## Who Is Constant Contact For?

**Best for:**
