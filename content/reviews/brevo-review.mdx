## What Is Brevo?

Brevo is a email marketing tool designed to help businesses build and engage their email audience. Best free/affordable email marketing platform for growing businesses.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** Best free/affordable email marketing platform for growing businesses — generous limits and multi-channel support

## Key Features

Brevo offers a suite of features focused on its core email marketing use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — generous free plan (300 emails/day).
- **Integrations:** Good transactional email.
- **Reporting:** Affordable paid plans.
- **Usability:** Generally well-designed with a reasonable learning curve..

## Pricing

Brevo starts at **$0/month** with a free plan. Pricing scales based on contact count and sending volume.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Landing page builder is basic
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- The free plan is genuinely limited — most real workflows require paid plans

## Who Is Brevo For?

**Best for:**
- Teams that prioritize generous capabilities
- Businesses already using complementary tools in the same ecosystem
- Organizations with the technical resources to manage the moderate learning curve

**Not ideal for:**
- Daily sending limits on free plan situations
- Teams looking for an all-in-one solution
- Businesses that want simpler, more direct value from their tools
