**Our verdict:** Best-looking emails in the industry — perfect for visual brands but limited on automation depth

Flodesk offers a suite of features focused on its core email marketing use case:

## Pricing

Flodesk starts at **$38/month**. Pricing scales based on contact count and sending volume.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- No SMS
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount
