## What Is Freshsales?

Freshsales is a crm sales automation tool designed to help businesses manage customer relationships and sales pipelines. Solid and affordable CRM with good AI features.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** Solid and affordable CRM with good AI features — best value choice in the mid-market segment

## Key Features

Freshsales offers a suite of features focused on its core crm sales automation use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — built-in phone and email.
- **Integrations:** Affordable pricing.
- **Reporting:** Good mobile app.
- **Usability:** Requires patience — the UI has rough edges that slow workflows..

## Pricing

Freshsales starts at **$15/month**. Pricing scales based on number of users and contacts.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Limited integrations outside Freshworks suite
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount

## Who Is Freshsales For?

**Best for:**
- Teams that prioritize built-in capabilities
- Businesses already using complementary tools in the same ecosystem
- Organizations with the technical resources to manage the moderate learning curve

**Not ideal for:**

- Teams looking for an all-in-one solution
- Businesses that want simpler, more direct value from their tools
