## What Is Keap?

Keap is a email marketing tool designed to help businesses build and engage their email audience. Powerful all-in-one for small businesses but brutally expensive and complex for what most need.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** Powerful all-in-one for small businesses but brutally expensive and complex for what most need

## Key Features

Keap offers a suite of features focused on its core email marketing use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — crm + email + payments combined.
- **Integrations:** Built for service businesses.
- **Reporting:** Good support.
- **Usability:** Requires patience — the UI has rough edges that slow workflows..

## Pricing

Keap starts at **$299/month**. Pricing scales based on contact count and sending volume.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Steep learning curve
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount

## Who Is Keap For?

**Best for:**
- Teams that prioritize crm capabilities

- Organizations with the technical resources to manage the significant learning curve

**Not ideal for:**
