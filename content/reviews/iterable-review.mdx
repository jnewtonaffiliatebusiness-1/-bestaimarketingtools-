## What Is Iterable?

Iterable is a email marketing tool designed to help businesses build and engage their email audience. Enterprise-grade cross-channel marketing automation.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** Enterprise-grade cross-channel marketing automation — powerful, but priced and scoped for large teams only

## Key Features

Iterable offers a suite of features focused on its core email marketing use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — best enterprise-grade automation.
- **Integrations:** Excellent data integration.
- **Reporting:** Highly scalable.
- **Usability:** Generally well-designed with a reasonable learning curve..

## Pricing

Iterable starts at **$500/month**. Pricing scales based on contact count and sending volume.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Requires dedicated ops/engineering
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount

## Who Is Iterable For?

**Best for:**
- Teams that prioritize best capabilities
- Businesses already using complementary tools in the same ecosystem
- Organizations with the technical resources to manage the moderate learning curve

**Not ideal for:**

- Teams looking for an all-in-one solution
- Businesses that want simpler, more direct value from their tools
