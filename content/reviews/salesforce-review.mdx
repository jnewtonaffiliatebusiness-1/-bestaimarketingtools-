## What Is Salesforce?

Salesforce is a crm sales automation tool designed to help businesses manage customer relationships and sales pipelines. The enterprise standard.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** The enterprise standard — infinitely customizable and powerful, but overkill and overpriced for SMBs

## Key Features

Salesforce offers a suite of features focused on its core crm sales automation use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — infinite customization.
- **Integrations:** Industry-standard for enterprise.
- **Reporting:** Excellent reporting and forecasting.
- **Usability:** Generally well-designed with a reasonable learning curve..

## Pricing

Salesforce starts at **$25/month**. Pricing scales based on number of users and contacts.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Very expensive at scale
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount

## Who Is Salesforce For?

**Best for:**
- Teams that prioritize infinite capabilities
- Businesses already using complementary tools in the same ecosystem
- Organizations with the technical resources to manage the moderate learning curve

**Not ideal for:**
- Extremely complex — requires dedicated admin situations
- Teams looking for an all-in-one solution
- Businesses that want simpler, more direct value from their tools
