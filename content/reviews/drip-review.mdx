## What Is Drip?

Drip is a email marketing tool designed to help businesses build and engage their email audience. Solid ecommerce email automation with good Shopify support, but Klaviyo has clearly won this race.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** Solid ecommerce email automation with good Shopify support, but Klaviyo has clearly won this race

## Key Features

Drip offers a suite of features focused on its core email marketing use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — good ecommerce automation.
- **Integrations:** Visual workflow builder.
- **Reporting:** Good A/B testing.
- **Usability:** Requires patience — the UI has rough edges that slow workflows..

## Pricing

Drip starts at **$39/month**. Pricing scales based on contact count and sending volume.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Limited SMS options
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount
