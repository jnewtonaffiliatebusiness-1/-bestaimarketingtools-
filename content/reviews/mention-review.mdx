## What Is Mention?

Mention is a social media analytics tool designed to help businesses manage and grow their social media presence. Solid mid-market social listening tool.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** Solid mid-market social listening tool — not as deep as Brandwatch but accessible for growing businesses

## Key Features

Mention offers a suite of features focused on its core social media analytics use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — good social listening coverage.
- **Integrations:** Email alerts and reporting.
- **Reporting:** Competitive monitoring.
