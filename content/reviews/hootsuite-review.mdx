## What Is Hootsuite?

Hootsuite is a social media analytics tool designed to help businesses manage and grow their social media presence. Most established social media platform, but has priced itself into irrelevance for most small businesses.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** Most established social media platform, but has priced itself into irrelevance for most small businesses
