**Our verdict:** All-in-one CRM with payments for service businesses — powerful concept, brutal price, complex interface

Keap CRM offers a suite of features focused on its core crm sales automation use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — crm + email + payments in one.

## Pricing

Keap CRM starts at **$299/month**. Pricing scales based on number of users and contacts.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Very steep learning curve
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount
