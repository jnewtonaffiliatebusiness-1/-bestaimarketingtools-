## What Is GetResponse?

GetResponse is a email marketing tool designed to help businesses build and engage their email audience. Surprisingly full-featured platform at an affordable price.

In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** Surprisingly full-featured platform at an affordable price — good for businesses wanting email, landing pages, and webinars in one

## Key Features

GetResponse offers a suite of features focused on its core email marketing use case:

- **Core Functionality:** The platform's primary strength is its main workflow — webinar hosting built in.
- **Integrations:** Affordable pricing.
- **Reporting:** AI email generator.
- **Usability:** Requires patience — the UI has rough edges that slow workflows..

## Pricing

GetResponse starts at **$19/month**. Pricing scales based on contact count and sending volume.

**Key pricing considerations:**
- Automation less powerful than ActiveCampaign
- Additional features may require higher-tier plans
- Annual billing typically offers a 15-20% discount
