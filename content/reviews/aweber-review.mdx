In this review, we'll cover everything you need to know before spending a dollar: what it does, how it's priced, where it shines, and where it falls short.

**Our verdict:** The original email marketing platform — free plan is decent, but growth has stalled vs. modern alternatives

AWeber offers a suite of features focused on its core email marketing use case:
